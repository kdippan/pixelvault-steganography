The Science of Secure Image Steganography

Least Significant Bit (LSB) Encoding

PixelVault utilizes advanced LSB steganography to embed your text directly into the binary color data of the image. By altering only the final bit of the RGB channels, the digital footprint is imperceptible to the human eye.

AES-256 Web Crypto API Security

Before embedding, your text undergoes rigorous encryption using the browser's native Web Crypto API. Protected by AES-GCM 256-bit standards, your secret message remains an unbreakable ciphertext without the correct password.

Zero-Trust, 100% Client-Side

Your privacy is absolute. Utilizing the HTML5 Canvas, PixelVault performs all cryptographic and steganographic processing locally on your device. No images, passwords, or messages ever traverse a remote server.
